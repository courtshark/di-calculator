Methodology & Resources ▸

How DI Is Flagged

PPG-1 = subgroup rate − all other rate

For positive outcomes, disproportionate impact is flagged when PPG-1 ≤ −E.

E = max(1.96 × sqrt(p̂(1−p̂)/n), 2.0%)

Quick Notes

  • Groups with n ≤ 10 are marked as insufficient data.
  • Each analysis cell shows the all-other rate, margin of error, and gap-closing estimate.
  • Counts must follow successes ≤ total count.
